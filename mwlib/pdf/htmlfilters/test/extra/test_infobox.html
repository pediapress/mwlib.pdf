P. V. Abdul Wahab is a social activist and a politician hailing from Kerala state in India. He is currently serving as the Member of Parliament (Rajyasabha) representing Kerala state. He is one of the top leaders of Indian Union Muslim League and the present Treasurer of the party. He has been elected unopposed as Member of Parliament twice to the Rajya Sabha, the Upper House of the Indian Parliament for the years 2004 to 2010[1] and 2015 to the present.[2]
